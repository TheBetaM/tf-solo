Licensing Information

The following applies only to files specifically included in the mod.

MAPS

Maps (.bsp files) original to the mod are free to use and modify within the context of the mod itself.
Permission is required to include or modify the map files outside the mod, in other mods, and the base game.

LICENSED MAPS

Maps (.bsp files) that are licensed for the mod (credited as COMMUNITY MAPS) are owned by their respective creators.
If available, check the individual map's licensing information at its source for more information.

ASSETS

Assets (any non-.bsp files) original to the mod are free to use and modify within the context of the mod itself.
Permission is required to include or modify the asset files outside the mod, in other mods, and the base game.

LICENSED ASSETS

Assets (any non-.bsp files) that are licensed for the mod (credited as COMMUNITY ASSETS) are owned by their respective creators.
Licensed assets are free to use in maps within the context of the mod itself. (check asset license for permission to modify)
If available, check the individual asset's licensing information at its source for more information.
